Pumpkin Pie

Description

The good old, classic, creamy, delicious pumpkin pie that goes well for dessert with any holiday(though most commonly found during Thanksgiving)

Ingredients

Pastry for the Single-Crust Pie:
  • 1 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup shortening
  • 3 ½ tablespoons cold water
Filling:
  • (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Prepare Mashed Pumpkin
  2. Preheat Oven
  3. Prepare Pie Crust
  4. Form Pie Crust
  5. Make Pie Filling
  6. Bake Pie
